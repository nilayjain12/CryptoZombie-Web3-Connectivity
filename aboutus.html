About Us

Welcome to our DAPP, a minimalistic web view of the "CryptoZombie" application that uses blockchain technologies to provide users with a unique experience. Our DAPP offers a variety of features that users can perform, such as creating a zombie, displaying the zombie details, zombie feeding on a kitty, zombie factory to create multiple random zombies at a time, and leveling up the zombie.

Our DAPP is hosted on a local host server, ensuring that users can access it easily and securely. We use 'Ganache' for the back-end, which guarantees reliable and efficient performance.

The features of our DAPP are written using 'Solidity' language as smart contracts. This ensures that the application is highly secure and that transactions are processed efficiently.

In addition to offering unique features, we also provide access to detailed documentation about solidity and ganache through our web app. This enables users to gain a better understanding of the technologies that power our DAPP and how they can leverage them to create their own blockchain-based applications.

At our DAPP, we are committed to providing users with a seamless and secure experience. Whether you are a novice or an experienced developer, our DAPP has something for everyone. Join us today and experience the power of blockchain technology!
